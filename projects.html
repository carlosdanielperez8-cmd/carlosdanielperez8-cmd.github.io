3D Banff Model - GAEA Terrain Simulation

Objective: Create a realistic 3D terrain model using a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) as the primary data source. The selected study area was Banff, chosen for its impressive geography, complex hydrology, and high scenic value.

The DEM was initially processed in ArcGIS Pro and later developed in Gaea, where node-based workflows and simulation techniques were used to generate a realistic representation of the terrain and water bodies in the region.
